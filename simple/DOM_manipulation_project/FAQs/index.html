Frequently Asked Questions

what is the return policy?
The exchanged product must be in its original condition, accompanied by the original invoice and any accompanying certificates or documents. Exchange facility without deducting making charge is available for the unused Gold/ Diamond Jewellery, within 14 days of purchase.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept payments via credit card, debit card, net banking, UPI, and popular digital wallets. Cash payments are also accepted at our physical stores.
How can I track my order?
You can track your order by visiting the "Track Order" section on our website and entering your order ID. Updates will also be sent to your registered email and phone number.
Is there a warranty on products?
Yes, all our products come with a standard warranty. The warranty period and details vary depending on the product and will be mentioned on the product page and invoice.
Can I cancel my order?
Yes, orders can be canceled before they are dispatched. To cancel your order, please contact our customer support team with your order ID. Refunds, if applicable, will be processed within 7 business days.
What should I do if I receive a damaged product?
If you receive a damaged product, please report it to our customer support within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange for a replacement or a refund based on your preference.
